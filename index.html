#OneWorldConference

The focus is on sustainability.

The #OneWorldConference aims to assemble oikos members and enthusiastic participants from around the world for an eventful conference on the importance of Sustainable Living. This conference will bring together key stakeholders involved in securing a sustainable future including companies, entrepreneurs and professors, since the very idea of a sustainable mindset begins with awareness and action.

Next

Sustainable Everything

The oikos Asia Meet 2016 aims to unite chapters from around the world for a conference oriented on practical learning and knowledge application. Participants from multi-disciplinary backgrounds from different parts of the world are united under one roof to help foster their speculative and rational minds into fruitful discussions and outcomes.

Each day is designed to have a specific theme and agenda to bring the best out of this multi-disciplinary group of individuals in a non-contemporary fashion. The three days have been planned to bring out the best in people with regard to Sustainable Energy, Sustainable Living and Sustaintable Entrepreneurship.

A message from Anita Negri, President, oikos International

"The oikos Asia Meet has proven to be an important project since it was founded in 2012. After the presence of this project in several cities, I am delighted that it will occur in Vellore this year. Vellore will be a wonderful place to engulf participants into the sustainability lifestyle other than just the talking. Organizing the Asia Meet is a learning-by-doing process which will allow organizers and participants to move into different spheres of sustainability as a whole. The Asia Meet is an experience that the people involved in will bring with them for longer in their lives. It will inspire, advocate for transformation and equip, beyond oikos members, society with ideas and actions to make the world the best place to strive in. We will make Vellore the place where participants will proactively understand some essential realms of what they can do to improve sustainability!"
